About MoveWell & Our Approach to Safety

Learn about our focus, our methods, and our commitment to your well-being.

Our Focus is Your Movement.

At MoveWell, we specialize in hands-on bodywork designed to help you recover, manage muscle tension, and improve your overall movement. Our goal is to support your active lifestyle by focusing on the health of your muscles and connective tissues.

It's important to understand what we are not. We are not a medical clinic, and our services do not replace physiotherapy or other medical treatments. Our practitioners are not doctors.

Our work is a proactive and complementary part of your wellness routine, helping to maintain your body's function and prevent the buildup of tension from daily life and activity.

We Do Not Treat Acute Injuries

Our services are not suitable for acute injuries. If you have recently been injured, please consult a qualified medical provider. Premature bodywork can interfere with the natural healing process.

Important Considerations & Contraindications

Please inform us if you have any of the following conditions, as we will need to modify your session or recommend that you consult a doctor before booking.

  • Recent Surgery: Especially if an area requires immobilization.
  • Open Wounds or Sutures: We will avoid the affected area completely.
  • Active Muscle Spasms: We must avoid stretching muscles that are in spasm.
  • Recent Muscle Strains: Strains should not be stretched within 72 hours of injury.
  • Diseases Affecting Tissues: Conditions like rheumatoid arthritis require special care.
  • Vascular Injury or Anticoagulants: Clearance from your doctor is required.
  • Active Infections: To avoid spreading the infection or causing tissue damage.
  • Excessive Pain During Movement: This may indicate an underlying condition that needs medical review.
  • Inflammation or Joint Effusion (Swelling): Aggressive bodywork can cause further damage.

If you have any questions or concerns about a medical condition, please speak with your doctor before scheduling a session. We are happy to work with your healthcare provider to ensure our services are a safe and effective part of your wellness plan.
